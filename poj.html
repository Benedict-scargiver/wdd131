About

Founded in 2022, The Game Store began as a small, local business aimed at providing the latest titles for PS5, Nintendo Switch, and Xbox Series X/S. Its founder, Sarah Williams, was passionate about gaming and saw a gap in the market for a store focused solely on current-gen consoles. With just one location, the store quickly attracted a loyal customer base by offering the newest games and personalized service.

As the gaming community grew, so did The Game Store. It expanded its inventory to include accessories and exclusive merchandise for the three major consoles. The store also became known for hosting small gaming events, which helped it build a strong connection with local players and families. This community-driven approach set it apart from larger retailers.

To adapt to the increasing trend of online shopping, The Game Store launched a website in its second year, allowing customers to browse and order games online for in-store pickup or delivery. The store also introduced a trade-in program, letting customers exchange old games for store credit, which became a popular feature and boosted customer retention.

Today, The Game Store operates multiple locations, maintaining its focus on the three major gaming platforms. By staying current with new releases and creating a welcoming in-store experience, the business continues to thrive despite the challenges of a rapidly changing retail environment.
